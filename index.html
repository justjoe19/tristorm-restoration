Our Story

At TriStorm Restoration, we're dedicated to helping home and business owners navigate property damage with honesty, compassion, and dependable care. With strong backgrounds as business owners in industry-related fields, we bring experience, integrity, and leadership to every project. After seeing firsthand how overwhelming and confusing the restoration process can be, we set out to build a company rooted in faith, transparency, and genuine care.

Our team met through our daughters' cheerleading gym, and we're all big-time cheer families who love showing up to competitions and cheering our girls on every step of the way. That shared passion and connection helped shape the heart behind TriStorm. We proudly serve Michiana and support our community with care you can trust
